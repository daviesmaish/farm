Welcome to our farm's webpage! We are excited to share with you all the wonderful things our farm has to offer We specilaize in sustainable and organic farming practices which means that we priolitize the health and wellbeing of our land, animals and our customers.On our farm, we grow a wide variety of fresh produce including fruits and vegetables.We use natural fertilizers and pest control methods to ensure our crops are healthy and free from harmful chemicals.In addition to produce, we also raise livesock such as cows,pigs and chicken.Our animals arewell treated, free to roam and graze in spacoius pastures.We believe that everyone should have access to fresh,healthy and locally grown food, which is why we sell our products at our on-site farm store and at local farmers markets.Thank you for visiting our website and we hope to see you soon at our farm!

Poultry

We supply 12 different breeds of point of lay hybrid pulletts which we sell from 16 weeks old onwards and these pullets are vaccinated against all major diseases and variant strains.We keep all 9 breeds together in a free range system which demonstrates how suitable they are for a mixed group and how well suited they are for a free range range egg production.We have these pullets available all year and we are happy to offer advice and and answer any queries you may have so please feel freee to give us a ring to arrange an apppointment to come and see our birds.

Merino sheep

Merino, breed of fine-wool sheep originating from spain; it was known as early as 12th century and may have been a Moorish importation. It is particularly well adapted to semiarid climates and to nomadic pasturing.The breed has become prominent in many countries wordwide.

Dorper sheep

Lamb, hogget and mutton are the meat of domestic sheep at different ages.A sheeep in its first year is called a lamb.One of the earliest animals to be domesticated for agricultural purposes , sheep are raised for meat.A sheep's wool is the most widely used animal fiber and is usually harvested by shearing .Dorper sheep produces best quality of this.

Sustainable farming practices

This refers to farming that promote ecological balance,conserve natural resources and minimize the impact on the environment.Our farm achieves this by incorporating the folllowing practices:

  • Conservation tillage
  • crop rotation
  • cover cropping
  • integrated pest management
  • organic farming
  • agroforestry
  • precision farming.

Farm Events

Farm to -table- dinners.This are events where the guests can enjoy meal made entirely from ingradients grown or raised on the farm.In addition we also offer agritourism, livestock shows,educational workshops,farm tours and farm fundraisers

Farm seasons

  • Planting in our farm begins during spring.
  • Irrigation and weeding takes place during summer
  • In this period of summer also we harvest most of our fruits and vegeatables.
  • Planning for the next growing season happpens during winter.
  • Focusing on and care and maintaining facilities also takes place during this season of winter.

Community involvement

  • Community Supported Agriculture(CSA) where members of the community purchase a share of farm's produce in advanceand in return they receive regular deliveries of fresh grown produce.
  • Volonteer opportunities; where we offer oppportunities for community members to help with tasks such as harvesting, planting and caring for animals.We also offer job opportunities for skilled, non-skilled and professionals.
